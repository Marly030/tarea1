Our Pokemon

Charmander

Charmander is a Fire-type Pokémon known for the flame that constantly burns at the tip of its tail. It's a small, dinosaur-like creature with orange skin and blue-green eyes.

Squirtle

Squirtle is a Water-type Pokémon known for its distinctive water cannons on its back. These cannons, also known as "squirtle shells," shoot water with great precision.

Bulbasaur

Bulbasaur is a dual-type Grass/Poison Pokémon known for the plant bulb on its back that grows into a large plant as it evolves.
